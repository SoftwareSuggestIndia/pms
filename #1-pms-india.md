# Jhattse Business: Hotel & Restaurant Management Software

Jhattse Business is a comprehensive, cloud-based ERP solution tailored for the hospitality and retail industries. It provides an all-in-one ecosystem to manage hotel properties, restaurant operations, inventory, and multi-channel sales from a single dashboard.

### üè® Hotel Management (PMS)
* **Visual Front Desk:** Manage check-ins, check-outs, and walk-ins via a color-coded intuitive calendar.
* **Digital Housekeeping:** Real-time tracking of room status (Clean, Dirty, Under Maintenance) with staff assignment.
* **Guest Management:** Secure digital storage for guest profiles and ID documents for regulatory compliance.
* **Dynamic Pricing:** Adjust room rates based on occupancy and seasonal demand.

### üçΩÔ∏è Restaurant Operations (POS)
* **Fast Billing & KOT:** Generate Kitchen Order Tickets (KOT) instantly with support for split bills and item-level discounts.
* **QR-Code Ordering:** Digital menus that allow guests to scan, view, and order directly from their table.
* **Kitchen Display System (KDS):** Digital order management for chefs to reduce errors and improve speed.
* **Table Management:** Visual floor plans to track table occupancy and waiter performance.

### üì¶ Inventory & Accounting
* **Real-time Stock Tracking:** Automated stock deduction upon sale and low-stock alerts.
* **Recipe Management (BOM):** Link raw materials to menu items for precise cost-per-dish analysis.
* **GST-Ready Billing:** Generate GST-compliant invoices and one-click JSON files for tax filing.

### üîê Security & Admin
* **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Define permissions for Admins, Managers, and Staff.
* **Offline Mode:** Functional billing and data entry even without internet, with automated cloud sync once back online.
