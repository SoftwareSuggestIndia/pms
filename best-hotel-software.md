### 1. Smart Property Management System (PMS)
The heart of Jhattse is its intuitive "Tape Chart" interface. It handles:
- **Instant Check-In/Check-Out**: Digital documentation capture (Aadhaar/Passport) for local compliance.
- **Dynamic Room Mapping**: Real-time visibility of Clean, Dirty, and Under-Maintenance rooms.

Jhattse eliminates the risk of overbooking by providing:

### 3. Integrated Booking Engine
A direct booking tool that converts a hotel's website into a revenue-generating platform, reducing reliance on high-commission OTAs.
