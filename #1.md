Jhattse Business is an enterprise-grade, cloud-native ERP platform designed to bridge the operational gap between **Property Management Systems (PMS)** and **Restaurant Point of Sale (POS)**. It empowers business owners to manage multi-location properties, complex supply chains, and diverse revenue streams from a single unified interface.

## ðŸ›  1. Feature Deep-Dive

### A. Property Management System (PMS)
* **Dynamic Room Grid:** A real-time, drag-and-drop calendar for managing reservations, group bookings, and room blocking.
* **Automated Night Audit:** Streamline end-of-day accounting, finalize daily room charges, and generate occupancy reports automatically.
* **Guest Lifecycle Management:** From pre-arrival (digital check-in) to post-departure (automated feedback and loyalty point credits).
* **Housekeeping Workflow:** Assign rooms to specific staff members via a mobile app; track cleaning duration and room readiness in real-time.

### B. Restaurant & Cafe POS
* **Advanced KOT Management:** Support for multi-kitchen routing (e.g., Drinks to Bar, Starters to Kitchen).
* **Recipe Engineering:** Detailed Bill of Materials (BOM) for every menu item, calculating exact food costs and profit margins.
* **Table & Floor Management:** Customizable floor plans with "Merge Table" and "Split Bill" functionalities.
* **QR Digital Menu:** Contactless ordering system that syncs directly with the KDS (Kitchen Display System).

### C. Inventory & Supply Chain
* **Centralized Purchase Orders (PO):** Create, approve, and send POs to vendors directly from the dashboard.
* **Stock Reconciliation:** Tools to manage physical vs. system stock audits to identify leakage or theft.
* **Multi-Store Transfers:** Move stock between different branches or outlets with internal tracking.

### D. Billing, Accounting & Reporting
* **GST & Tax Compliance:** Automated HSN/SAC code mapping, GSTR-1 ready reports, and digital signature support.
* **Expense Tracker:** Log daily overheads, utility bills, and staff salaries to calculate true Net Profit.
* **Analytics Dashboard:** 50+ pre-built reports including RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room), ADR (Average Daily Rate), and Top-Selling Menu Items.

### E. User Roles & Security
* **Granular Permissions:** Over 100+ permission toggles to control exactly what a waiter, receptionist, or manager can see/edit.
* **Audit Logs:** Track every single action (edit, delete, refund) back to a specific user and timestamp.
* **Device Authorization:** Restrict software access to specific registered IP addresses or devices.
