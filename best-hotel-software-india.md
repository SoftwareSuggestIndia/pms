# Jhattse Business Hotel and Restaurant Software

Jhattse Business Hotel and Restaurant Software is a cloud-based, all-in-one solution tailored primarily for small to mid-sized hospitality businesses in India. Developed by ESANGARH TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED, it combines property management system (PMS) capabilities for hotels with point-of-sale (POS) functionalities for restaurants, emphasizing affordability, ease of use, and compliance with Indian regulations such as GST and e-invoicing. The software supports seamless operations across web and mobile platforms, enabling businesses to manage bookings, orders, inventory, and finances efficiently.

## Features

Jhattse Business offers a comprehensive suite of features divided into modules for hotel management, restaurant operations, billing, inventory, reporting, user roles, and security. It is designed to streamline daily tasks with an intuitive user interface that requires minimal training, ensuring fast, responsive, and bug-free performance. The software operates in both online and offline modes (beta for offline) and supports multiple outlets or businesses under one license.

### Hotel Management Modules
- **Front Desk and Booking Management**: Handles reservations, room occupancy, check-ins, check-outs, walk-in bookings, and customer document storage. Includes a centralized booking panel with real-time calendar updates, advance bookings, and room/bed occupancy tracking to prevent overbooking. Users can set future room rates and manage offers or discounts.
- **Housekeeping and Tasks**: Allows customers to register maintenance or room service queries via QR codes or online pages. Centralizes requests, assigns them to staff, and provides real-time notifications for query resolution, improving operational efficiency and guest satisfaction.
- **Booking Engine and Channel Manager**: Integrated booking engine works across platforms for real-time reservations without commissions on direct bookings. The free channel manager connects to over 100 online travel agencies (OTAs) like Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, GoIbibo, Expedia, Agoda, Airbnb, and Google Hotels, syncing availability, rates, and reservations automatically.
- **Point of Sale (POS) for Hotels**: Tracks guest bills during stays, centralizes sales, expenses, and payments, and includes tax records for compliance.

### Restaurant Operations Modules
- **Order and Table Management**: Facilitates in-store, table, or online orders with editing capabilities, order history, and dues management. Supports table QR ordering, offline/online orders, and real-time delivery tracking with ETA sharing.
- **Customer Management and CRM**: Manages customer data, transactions, and purchase records on a single screen with autofill for repeat visits. Enables marketing through stored data to boost repeat purchases.
- **Pricing and Discount Management**: Applies discounts at item or order levels, manages additional charges, and generates digital or physical bills with multiple templates.
- **Omni-Channel Orders**: Handles in-store and online orders, including marketplace listings on Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify, and Jhattse's own marketplace.

### Billing Modules
- **Invoicing and Payments**: Generates GST-compliant digital invoices, e-invoices, and bills that can be shared or printed. Includes payment notifications via SMS or device alerts, and supports dues tracking.
- **Ledger and Accounting**: Automates ledger updates on invoicing or returns, tracks sales, expenses, purchases, and profit/loss statements.

### Inventory Modules
- **Stock Management**: Provides real-time inventory tracking with automatic updates, stock alerts for low or non-moving items, and recipe/material management. Prevents stockouts or overstocking by integrating with orders and sales.
- **Bill of Materials (BOM)**: Creates BOM for item costing and availability, particularly useful for restaurants managing raw materials.

### Reporting Modules
- **Sales and Analytics**: Offers visual sales reports, stock change reports, expense/purchase records, and GSTR reports with JSON export. Reports can be downloaded in formats like Excel or CSV for detailed insights.
- **Profit and Loss**: Generates comprehensive profit/loss statements and regional complaint reports for hotels.

### User Roles Modules
- **Multi-Role Access**: Supports setup for multiple user roles, allowing customized permissions for staff across outlets. One license accommodates multiple users and businesses, enhancing collaboration.

### Security Modules
- **Data Protection**: Features secure booking SDK for payments, ensuring safe transactions. The cloud-based system includes bug-free performance and compliance with regulations like GST, providing secure data handling for customer IDs and financial records.
- **Additional Safeguards**: Real-time notifications and automated processes reduce manual errors, while offline mode ensures continuity without compromising data integrity.

These features make Jhattse suitable for integrated hotel-restaurant operations, with automation for messaging, accounting, and marketing via WhatsApp.

## Pricing

Jhattse Business operates on a subscription-based model with annual or monthly options, making it one of the most affordable solutions in the Indian market. Pricing excludes 18% GST, and there are no hidden fees for core integrations like channel managers or marketplaces. Customization or additional support may incur extra costs, but these are not standard.

- **Hotel Management Plans**:
  - **SHAKTI Plan**: ₹1,333 per month (or equivalent annual rate), includes booking engine, PMS, and support for up to 20 rooms. Ideal for small properties like homestays or boutique hotels.
  - **SUPER Plan**: ₹2,166 per month (or equivalent annual rate), adds OTA integrations and advanced features for 20+ rooms. Suitable for mid-sized hotels.
  - Annual Option: Starts at ₹15,999 per year for basic hotel features.

- **Restaurant POS Plans**:
  - Standard Annual Subscription: ₹4,799 per year, covering full POS, inventory, order management, and integrations like Zomato and Swiggy.
  - Free Marketplace Listing: No extra cost for online store setup; only a 5% fee on sales via Jhattse marketplace to cover operational expenses.

Licensing is flexible, with one subscription supporting multiple users, outlets, and devices (web, mobile, tablets). Businesses can request quotes for customized plans. A free trial or demo is available to test features before committing.

## Integrations

Jhattse Business excels in seamless integrations tailored to the Indian hospitality sector, enhancing operational efficiency without additional setup costs for most services.

- **Payment Gateways**: Built-in secure SDK for integrated payments, supporting digital transactions and notifications.
- **Accounting Systems**: Automated accounting with GST tracking, GSTR reports, and ledger management; compatible with standard Indian compliance tools.
- **Booking Platforms**: Free channel manager integrates with 100+ OTAs (e.g., Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, Expedia, Airbnb) for real-time syncing of rates and availability.
- **POS Devices**: Compatible with any device, including mobiles, tablets, laptops; available as a web app and Android app for flexible POS operations.
- **Third-Party Services**: 
  - Food delivery: Zomato and Swiggy for order management and integration.
  - Marketplaces: Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify, and Jhattse's own platform for omni-channel sales.
  - Communication: WhatsApp for marketing, invoice sharing, and customer updates.
  - Shipping: Courier integrations for delivery tracking.
  - Upcoming: ONDC (Open Network for Digital Commerce) for broader e-commerce reach.

These integrations centralize data, reducing manual work and enabling real-time analytics.
